Application Guide

Steps to Use the Application

  1. Step 1: Sign Up
    Create an App Track Account through the App Track team (help tab on home page).
  2. Step 2: Verify Email
    Check your inbox and confirm your email address to activate your account.
  3. Step 3: Log In
    Login with your account.
  4. Step 4: Explore Features
    Navigate through menus and try out the core functionalities of the app.
  5. Step 5: Get Support
    If you encounter issues, contact the App Track team. We value "person to person" assistance.
